allowed in both the base zoning district and the overlay district and must comply with
height, yard, area and parking requirements of the base zoning district.

nonconforming use by March 31, 2015. When counting the number of unrelated persons in
a single dwelling unit, persons related by blood, marriage or adoption shall count as one
