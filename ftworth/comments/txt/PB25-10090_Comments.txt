is obscured by a minimum six (6) foot masonry wall. "Masonry" shall include brick, stone,
cementitious fiber concrete products or stucco. Similar products made from such
materials and/or durable recycled material shall be allowed as approved by the Building
Official.

No attached garage facing a public or private street may project greater than eight (8) feet

property under unified ownership or control and used as one premise. The decision of the
city traffic engineer may be appealed to the city council. (Code 1964, § 26-102.3; Ord. No.
8288, § 1, 3-10-81)
